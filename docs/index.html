    音楽の街

    Music, Gear & Synths

    My pilgrimage list: Ochanomizu's guitar street, Five G for vintage poly, synth/gear shops, Disk Union to dig, and listening bars worth the trip. Go on a weekday — the good shops are tiny.

    ゲーム・アニメ・技術

    Games, Anime & Tech Culture

    Akihabara is home base. Super Potato for carts, GiGO for crane games, Nakano Broadway for the deep cuts. Budget a whole afternoon and bring cash.

    集まり・イベント

    My crowd. Tokyo Indies and AI Tinkerers for demo nights, Connpass/Doorkeeper for the rest, and the big cons — Comiket, TGS, AnimeJapan — when they land. Bring a build even if it's janky.
