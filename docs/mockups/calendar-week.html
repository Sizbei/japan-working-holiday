Why this beats a grid here

  • The bars are the value. Three beer gardens + the admin window + teamLab all overlap your arrival week — you see it in one glance, which a month grid hides and a 24-hour grid would leave 80% empty.
  • Drag-to-reschedule between days (the dashed teamLab Borderless chip shows the drop preview); click a day to quick-create an all-day event.
  • Month + Agenda stay — Week is a third lens, not a replacement.
  • No hourly grid, no timed events — cut as Notion-cosplay for an all-day trip calendar (per the adversarial review).
