## The two departures the week hinges on
- **Biei (20th):** the **08:12** Furano→Biei train, then the **09:26** Blue Pond bus.
- **Asahidake (21st):** the **07:19** Furano→Asahikawa train → **09:15** Ideyu-go bus (route 66) → arrive Asahidake **~11:03**. The next bus (13:15) leaves you rushing the mountain before the 18:00 last ropeway.

## The return leg (23rd) — how the beer garden connects
09:40 Ideyu-go bus Asahidake → Asahikawa (~11:18) → **~11:45 Limited Express Lilac/Kamui** → Sapporo **~13:10** (~1h25, ~¥4,690 non-reserved / ¥5,440 reserved; ~2/hour — reserve a seat in festival week). Check in near Susukino, walk ~10 min north to Odori. *(Fallback: the 11:40 bus still lands you in Sapporo mid-afternoon.)*

- **Ideyu-go bus (route 66)** (3/day, ¥1,800) — Asahikawa→Asahidake 07:15→09:03 / 09:15→11:03 / 13:15→15:03 · Asahidake→Asahikawa 09:40→11:18 / 11:40→13:18 / 15:40→17:18.
- **Asahidake Ropeway (summer)** — first up ~06:00, last down 18:00, every 15 min; round trip ¥3,500 (one-way uphill not sold after 16:00).

## Mountain day (22nd) — Nakadake Onsen loop
06:30 first ropeway → climb from Sugatami (1,600 m) → **Asahidake summit 2,291 m ~10:00** → traverse to **Nakadake Onsen ~12:10** (wild footbath in the rock) → back at Sugatami ~14:40 (~7–8 hr, ~11 km). **Fallbacks:** summit out-and-back (~5–6 hr); or an easy Sugatami + day-use onsen weather day. **Safety:** active volcano, fast weather; layers/water/food; upper scree; lower trail is bear country — carry a bell, check closures.

## Garlic (Diamond Ninniku)
Arai Farm is farm-stay + mail-order only (no walk-in). Buy at Biei Senka (check), the にんにく種蔵 shop in Sapporo (Tanukikōji), the **Biei Senka counter at New Chitose Airport** on the way out, or arai-farm.net.
